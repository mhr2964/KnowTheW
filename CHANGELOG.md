- **Unblock Heroku releases by caching computed PBP results instead of raw game summaries** — The Mongo `gameSummaries` cache had grown to 515 MB (1394 docs at ~370 KB each, full raw ESPN summary blobs) and pushed the free tier over its 512 MB quota, causing the release-phase `seed-distributions.js` script's `bulkWrite` to fail and every recent deploy to be aborted at the release stage. A one-off `scripts/drop-legacy-game-summaries.js` (run via `heroku run` post-deploy) idempotently drops the now-unused `gameSummaries` collection to reclaim quota. Net effect: total PBP cache size drops by orders of magnitude (~500 MB → under 10 MB at saturation), and cache-warming scripts can no longer block a release.
